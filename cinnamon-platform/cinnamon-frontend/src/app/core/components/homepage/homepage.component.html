Cinnamon is designed to anonymize your data by providing methods both for direct anonymization as well as data generation with synthesis methods.

The Application is suitable for experts and non-experts as it guides you through the process of protecting your data. Detailed explanations about steps, parameters and statistics are available throughout the application by clicking on the info icons .

The different steps of the workflow are shown in the navigation bar on left side of the screen. It visualizes the progress of anonymization process and allows to go back to completed steps at any time. The steps can be grouped into four categories:

  • Setup of the project
  • Upload of the data
  • Configuration and execution of the anonymization
  • Evaluation of the anonymized data
